Simple Bluetooth pairing

Set it up from your phone in minutes.

Open the ThisTime app, turn on the desk display, and connect over Bluetooth. Once paired, your phone becomes the control center for choosing the active countdown, reminders, checklist items, priority level, and display preferences.

  1. Power on ThisTime and keep it near your phone during first-time setup.
  2. Open the mobile app, tap “Add display,” and select your device from the Bluetooth list.
  3. Create or choose a deadline, then tap “Send to display” to sync the countdown instantly.
